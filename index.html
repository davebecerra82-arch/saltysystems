B2B SaaS Lead Scoring & Enrichment Pipeline

Nexen Capital needed a repeatable way to identify which B2B SaaS startups were worth pursuing. We designed and built a full enrichment and ICP scoring pipeline — pulling raw leads from Apollo, enriching through a Clay waterfall, scoring via Claude API against a weighted rubric, and exporting directly into HubSpot. Hard disqualifiers filtered out noise before scoring ran, keeping the output clean and actionable.
