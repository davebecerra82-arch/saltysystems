B2B SaaS Lead Scoring & Enrichment Pipeline Pipeline de Scoring y Enriquecimiento de Leads B2B SaaS

Nexen Capital needed a repeatable way to identify which B2B SaaS startups were worth pursuing. We designed and built a full enrichment and ICP scoring pipeline: pulling raw leads from Apollo, enriching through a Clay waterfall, scoring via Claude API against a weighted rubric, and exporting directly into HubSpot. Hard disqualifiers filtered out noise before scoring ran, keeping the output clean and actionable. Nexen Capital necesitaba una forma repetible de identificar qué startups B2B SaaS merecían la pena. Diseñamos y construimos un pipeline completo de enriquecimiento y scoring ICP: extrayendo leads de Apollo, enriqueciendo mediante un waterfall de Clay, puntuando con Claude API contra una rúbrica ponderada y exportando directamente a HubSpot. Los disqualificadores duros filtraron el ruido antes de que corriera el scoring, manteniendo el output limpio y accionable.
